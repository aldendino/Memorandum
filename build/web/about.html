About

Yu Yi

The Yu Yi Productions executive team are very passionate and happy about bringing this film to you. They are all talented individuals working together to create a film that is both visually and aurally stimulating while asking the viewer to question their reliance on memory and identity.

Crew

Adrienne McLaren Devenyi

Adrienne McLaren Devenyi is the director and the co-writer of Memorandum. One of her fondest memories is when the song “Steal My Sunshine” was the song of the summer. She was 6 years old and remembers cruising around downtown with her mom and not having a care in the world.

Memorandum is a slice-of-life narrative about the power of memory and what can become of a person when they can no longer make memories. Adrienne is so excited and motivated to be making a film built for the senses, and to make something as serious as Alzheimer’s come through in an honest yet beautifully composed way.

Brian Hongku Han

Brian Hongku Han is the digital media producer and post-production/editor for Memorandum film. He values the meaning of memories and how every piece of memories can impacts one's or community's pathway, and he joined the production to express his passion by putting creative inputs into production of Memorandum. Brian's most fondest memory would be the first day when he moved to Canada. The temperature, scent, snow, people, and even the background music at the airport... are the most memorable event that will remain forever in him as the first impression of Canada.

Duncan Lam

Duncan Lam is the web supervisor, responsible for the oversight of the website’s development and its content. Some of Duncan’s fondest memories were made with his fellow RTA-mates. Perhaps one of his fondest memories is when he went on a road trip over the summer with his close friends.

Isabelle Mendoza

Isabelle Mendoza is the co-producer and production manager of the short film, Memorandum. Her passion is creating and producing stories that explore human interactions and evoke drama and a sense of nostalgia. These elements are what drew her to work on this film. One of Isabelle’s fondest memories was when she travelled to LA for the first time. It was an experience that made her feel more independent, helped her discover her love for film and television as well as encouraging her to continue travelling abroad.

Jual DaCosta

Jual DaCosta is the Marketing and EPK Director of the short film, Memorandum. She is a RTA (Radio and Television Arts) student who has always had a love for every aspect of the media and film industry. Jual’s wish is to make a change through the work she does that’s the reason she is happy to work on the Yu Yi team to create Memorandum. Throughout her life Jual has garnered experiences and created memories that have helped mould her into the person she is today. Her fondest memory is when she had the chance to interview Tim Burton and he told her “I see a lot of knowledge, passion and depth in you. You can be anything you want, a director or producer or even a camera woman and you can do them all at the same time too.”

Madelaine Sawyers

Madelaine Sawyers is the Executive Producer and Co-Writer behind Memorandum. She is excited to work on a film that she believes tells an important story about family dynamics and how we define ourselves in and outside our family structure. One of Madelaine’s fondest memories is meeting her half-sister for the first time.
